little bit horrifying,
little bit relieving,
sometimes simple,
sometimes amusing,
like a huge piece of heart
just crying to implode,
like these roots now
crave for the air
and the leaves dig
their own grave,
seems like the world is ending,
little bit horrifying,

Ever heard about
the sometimes theory,
Those random thoughts
to define your story,
Like
sometimes I think
Why do they talk to me,
Is there a reason
or they are also lonely,
My heart wants attention
but my mind wants to be free,
Want to go everywhere
but I am stuck like a tree,
These sometimes were
the moments,
Recognized as my history,
The past that never listened
the orchestra of my mystery,
Always thought the future
will follow my past,
Coz in my sometimes
they are friends at last,
But there were some people
who made me proud,
Looking that inner beauty
in the miscellaneous crowd,
Which made my sometimes
water in drought.

The Sometimes Theory

फिर मैं आगे को निकल गया,
बदलता मौसम मुझे रोकने को रुका,
मैं घबराया पीछे मुड़ा,
मैं दौड़ा,
ज़मीन पीछे खींचने लगी,
मैं रुका,
आसमान गिरने को आ गया,
बदलता मौसम मुझे रोकने को रुका,
फिर मैं आगे को निकल गया |

I know you're there,
You are afraid,
Listening the limitless speech
behind my unbroken silence,
Noticing the warm tears
behind my determined eyes,
Feeling the broken soul
behind my concrete faith,
You are the only one
who knows the imaginary walls
between our infinite thoughts,
And now it's time to tell our untold
stories behind those unforgettable
memories.
